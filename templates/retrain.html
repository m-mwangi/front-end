Retrain Model

Upload new data and retrain your model for improved performance.

What is Model Retraining?

It refers to the process of updating an existing machine learning model by training it again on new or additional data. As your dataset grows or changes, retraining ensures the model remains accurate and relevant by learning from the latest trends and patterns.

How?

You will upload your dataset,and click on the button retrain model for it to retrain. Wait for a confirmation on the success of the process.

Why Retrain?

Over time, the performance of a model can degrade if it's not regularly updated with new data. Retraining your model allows it to adapt, improving its predictions and accuracy as new information becomes available. In our case new data can reveal emerging risks or patterns that were not captured before.

Get Started Now

Begin by uploading a new dataset(CSV ONLY with 7 columns) then click on the upload file button to upload the file. Finally click on the retrain model button for retraining.
